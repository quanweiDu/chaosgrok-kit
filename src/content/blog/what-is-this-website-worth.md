Most people hand over everything to platforms — their words, their readers, their income. WeChat, Weibo, Douyin. These platforms hold your audience hostage. They can take it back anytime.

大多数人把一切都交给了平台——文字、读者、收入。微信、微博、抖音。这些平台替你"保管"你的受众，随时可以收回。

This website is different.

这个网站不一样。

Every word published here stays here. No algorithm decides who sees it. No platform can delete it. The readers who find you here find *you* — not a version of you filtered through someone else's interests.

这里发布的每一个字都永久留在这里。没有算法决定谁能看到它。没有平台可以删除它。在这里找到你的读者，找到的是真正的你——不是经过别人利益过滤后的版本。

There is also something more practical: ownership.

还有一件更实际的事：所有权。

When you sell a book here, the money is yours. When you build a readership here, those readers are yours. When you write here, the words are yours — not licensed to a platform, not subject to terms of service that change without notice.

在这里卖书，钱是你的。在这里积累读者，读者是你的。在这里写作，文字是你的——不是授权给某个平台，不受随时可能变更的服务条款约束。

The internet is full of content that disappears. Published today, buried tomorrow, forgotten in a year.

互联网上充满了会消失的内容。今天发布，明天被淹没，一年后无人记得。

A website does not disappear. An essay written today can be found ten years from now, by someone who needs exactly those words, in exactly that moment.

网站不会消失。今天写下的文章，十年后还能被人找到——被某个恰好需要这些文字的人，在恰好需要的那一刻找到。

So what is this website worth?

所以这个网站有什么价值？

The website itself is worth nothing.

网站本身，什么价值都没有。

It is a container. A very good, very clean container that belongs entirely to you.

它是一个容器。一个非常好、非常干净、完全属于你的容器。

What you put inside it — that is where the value lives.

你放进去的东西，才是价值所在。
