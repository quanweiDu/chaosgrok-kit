极简不是一种风格，它是一种纪律。风格可以模仿，纪律需要实践。

When we talk about minimalism in design, we often mean the visual outcome — white space, clean lines, restrained palette. But the more interesting question is: what was removed, and why?

每一个被删掉的元素背后，都有一个决定。这个决定需要你清楚地知道：什么是核心，什么是装饰。大多数人弄不清楚这两者，所以他们的"极简"只是空洞。

The discipline is not in the subtraction itself, but in the clarity of judgment that makes subtraction possible.
