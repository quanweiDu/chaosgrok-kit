# 咨询页个人故事文案

---

## 中文版

我没有光鲜的履历。

大学白天睡觉，晚上泡酒吧。
毕业去了煤矿，三年。
又去测绘，三年。
创业，一年，失败。
工地劳资，两年。
开车，一年。
外卖，断断续续，一直跑。

2018年，我开始买显卡研究以太坊挖矿。
然后炒币、做空投、研究Web3、钻进区块链。
然后是AI，然后是代码。

钱砸进去了，时间砸进去了。
有人说我废了。

但我在那些年里学到了一件事——
**大多数人的困境，不是信息不够，是框架不清。**
他们不缺答案，他们缺的是把问题想清楚的能力。

我走过足够多的弯路，
才知道怎么帮人少走一些。

## 英文版

I don't have a clean résumé.

I slept through college and spent my nights at bars.
After graduation: three years in a coal mine.
Three years in land surveying.
One failed startup.
Two years managing labor on construction sites.
A year driving.
Deliveries, on and off, whenever I needed to.

In 2018, I started buying GPUs to mine Ethereum.
Then trading crypto, chasing airdrops, going deep into Web3 and blockchain.
Then AI. Then code — because nothing else closed the loop.

I burned through money. I burned through years.
People wrote me off.

But somewhere in all of it, I learned one thing clearly:
**Most people's problems aren't about lacking information.
They're about lacking a clear framework to see what's actually in front of them.**

I've walked enough wrong roads
to know how to help someone walk fewer of them.
