title: The Art of Less
titleZh: 少即是多的艺术
pubDate: 2026-03-08
---
Minimalism is not about having nothing. It's about making room for what matters.


极简主义并非一无所有，而是为了腾出空间留给真正重要的东西。



When we remove the unnecessary, the essential speaks. In web design, this means stripping away gradients, shadows, and heavy frameworks. It means trusting the typography and the whitespace.


去除不必要的元素，本质便会显现。在网页设计中，这意味着摒弃渐变、阴影和繁复的框架，转而信任字体和留白。


This blog itself is an experiment in reducing friction. Just pure HTML and CSS. No build tools, no complex CMS. Just writing.


这个博客本身就是一次减少摩擦的实验。只用了纯粹的 HTML 和 CSS。没有构建工具，没有复杂的 CMS。只有写作。
